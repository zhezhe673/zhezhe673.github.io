Research

Research Interests

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Southwestern University of Finance and Economics, advised by Prof. Fode Zhang. My research focuses on building more reliable learning systems through causal analysis, with particular interest in multimodal large language models (MLLMs) and transfer learning.
